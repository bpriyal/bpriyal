Poker Hands

Where counting is everything

A five-card hand is drawn from a standard 52-card deck. Each hand is a combination, not a sequence.

Modeling the Problem

The total number of possible hands is:

$$ \binom{52}{5} $$

Examples

Four of a Kind

$$ 13 \times 48 = 624 $$

Full House

$$ 13 \times \binom{4}{3} \times 12 \times \binom{4}{2} = 3744 $$
