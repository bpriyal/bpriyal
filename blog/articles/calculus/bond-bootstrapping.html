Model the Problem

Given market prices of coupon-bearing bonds \(P_{mkt}\), compute zero rates \(r(0,t)\): \[ P_{\text{model}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} c_i e^{-r(0,t_i) t_i} \]

Explain the Modelled Events

Bonds are bundles of cash flows at discrete maturities. Bootstrapping recursively solves for the unknown zero rate at each maturity.

Extrapolation (C++ Implementation)

  • Store zero rates in std::map<double,double> or std::vector<pair>
  • Loop over sorted bonds and use a root solver (Secant or Newton) to zero out price difference
